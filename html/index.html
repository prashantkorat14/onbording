What we do

Just make an upload page

Cloudtransfr lets anyone upload files to your Google Drive, Dropbox, or Microsoft Onedrive using a custom URL. This lets you receive big files from other people without showing them what else is in your cloud.

Cloudtransfr helps you collect documents, pictures and videos from anyone. You can even receive files your mail provider blocks like exe's.

Using your Cloudtransfr url, other people can upload files into a folder in your cloud storage account. Handy for teachers who want to collect student assignments, or businesses who want to receive suppliers’ quotes.

To use Cloudtransfr, just make an upload page and connect your cloud storage account.
